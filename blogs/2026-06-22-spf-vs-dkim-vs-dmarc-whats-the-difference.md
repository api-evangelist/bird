This Bird article explains three complementary email authentication standards: SPF authorizes sending servers via DNS, DKIM cryptographically signs messages, and DMARC enforces alignment between authentication and the visible From address while providing reporting. The post recommends implementing all three in sequence, starting with SPF and DKIM before adding DMARC in monitor mode.
